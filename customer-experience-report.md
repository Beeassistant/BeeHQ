# Customer Experience Report: Side Hustle Lab
## Persona: Gen Z Female (18-26), looking for extra income

---

## SECTION 1: CUSTOMER JOURNEY WALKTHROUGH

**Entry Point:** I found this via X/TikTok/Instagram ad or a friend shared it.

**First 30 seconds on site:**
- Hero says "Will your side hustle actually make money?" — immediately speaks to my anxiety
- Tagline "Built by an AI that never sleeps" — cool, different, I trust tech more than guru courses
- Stats show 20+ hustles researched — credible
- BUT: The main headline is about side hustles making money, not about MY goal of "earning my first $100" or "having my own business"

**The Instant Checker (the hook):**
- I type "UGC" or select from dropdown
- Get a verdict in seconds — THIS IS GOOD. Gen Z wants instant answers.
- But the result shows "Get UGC Report ($7)" — I'm not ready to pay yet. I want to know MORE first.
- The "Good Fit For" text is tiny and hard to read
- No clear "here's your first step" after reading the verdict

**Free Reports:**
- "No email required" — GENIUS. This removes friction.
- I click UGC report, read it, it's actually useful and honest
- BUT: There's no clear CTA at the end of the free report. What do I do next? How do I start?

**The Purchase Flow:**
- I want to buy the paid report
- Click "Get this report" → goes to Stripe
- I have to create a Stripe account or remember my password — FRICTION
- The $7 price is fine but I want to know WHAT I'm getting first

**After Purchase:**
- I'm redirected to a thank you page
- But I'm not sure where my report is
- Email arrives in 10 minutes — okay that's fine

## SECTION 2: CRITIQUES & GAPS

### 2.1 Relatability Issues
- **Testimonials**: "Marcus T.", "Jake R." — these sound like male corporate names. I'm a 22-year-old woman. Where are the testimonials from people like ME?
- **Personas**: "9-to-5ers wanting more" is vague. "Idea paralysis people" is a bit dismissive. None speak to my specific situation: working a job I hate, wanting to escape, not knowing where to start.
- **Visuals**: Images show diverse women but language throughout is very American/corporate. "Starbucks Frappuccino" alienates non-US customers.
- **No "me too" moment**: I don't see anyone who looks like me who has successfully done this.

### 2.2 Missing Features
- **No "Start Here" guide**: I land on the site, see 20+ options, and feel overwhelmed. There's no "if you're new, start here" path.
- **No community/social proof in real-time**: Static testimonials from strangers don't convince me. Where are the TikTok-style testimonials? The Instagram posts? The X threads?
- **No urgency or momentum builder**: The site is very static. No "5 people bought this in the last hour" or "this report was updated today with new 2026 data."
- **No clear next step after free report**: I read the free UGC report. It's helpful. But then what? I have to manually navigate back and figure out the next step.
- **No free preview of paid reports**: I want to see a sample of what $7 gets me before buying.

### 2.3 Conversion Gaps
- **No comparison table**: What's the difference between the $7 report and the $25 custom report? When would I pick one over the other?
- **The "Good Fit For" in checker results is too small** — this is actually a key decision driver for me
- **No limited-time framing**: Everything feels static. A "these 3 reports are the most bought this week" would create urgency.
- **The checkout flow requires Stripe login** — major friction for Gen Z who may not have a Stripe account set up

### 2.4 Content Gaps (vs. competitors)
- **No "Day in the Life" content**: I want to see what someone doing this side hustle actually does day-to-day
- **No risk-level indicator**: Some of these are "maybe" but WHAT'S the risk? Time? Money? Getting scammed?
- **No "vs. alternatives" comparison**: If I want to do UGC, should I also consider affiliate marketing? The site doesn't help me choose between options
- **No "how to make your first dollar" path**: The reports validate ideas but don't show the actual first step to earning

### 2.5 Language & Tone
- **"Side Hustle Lab" sounds corporate/clinical** — I want something that feels more like "my friend who helps me figure this out"
- **Too much focus on "saving money/time"** — I want to focus on MAKING money, not just avoiding mistakes
- **The guarantee is buried** — it should be more prominent to reduce my risk perception
- **"Built by an AI" is cool but also a bit cold** — I want to know there's a human backing this who cares

## SECTION 3: WHAT COMPETITORS OFFER THAT WE DON'T

1. **Niche建设的"success path"**: Other sites show a clear "Day 1 → Day 30 → Day 90" path
2. **Community/discord**: Others have communities where you can see real people succeeding
3. **Free mini-courses**: Some offer a free email course to "test drive" before buying
4. **"Similar to what you viewed"**: Recommendations based on what you just read
5. **ROI calculator**: "If you spend $7 on this report, you save X hours and don't make Y mistake"

## SECTION 4: PRIORITY FIXES (Quick Wins)

1. **Add relatable testimonials** from women (with first names, locations, relatable quotes)
2. **Add a "Start Here" or "Not Sure?" section** for overwhelmed visitors
3. **Show "Good Fit For" more prominently** in checker results (make it bigger, a different color)
4. **Add a "Most Popular This Week" badge** to create social proof urgency
5. **Add clearer CTAs throughout** — after free reports, suggest next steps
6. **Add comparison between $7 and $25 options** — help me choose
7. **Add a "What you'll learn in this report" preview** before the buy button
8. **Mobile navbar needs dropdown menu**
9. **Logo text should be "Side Hustle Lab" not split**
10. **Diversify testimonials** — include women, international, various income levels
